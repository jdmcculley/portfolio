Process

Feature Design Process

The digital product lifecycle is compressing. This is how I structure the process to move at that speed — with AI augmented at every phase and human judgment governing every decision.

Discovery Phase 1

Problem Definition

9
goals

PM works with stakeholders to understand business needs, customer pain points, and desired outcomes. Research validates the problem space.

► AI Augmentation
AI-Accelerated
AI Handles
  • Synthesize survey data & usage patterns
  • Map competitive landscape
  • Generate research outlines from raw context
Human Leads
  • Frame the right problem
  • Conduct stakeholder interviews
  • Apply judgment to what actually matters

"AI finds the haystacks. My researcher finds the needles."

► Key Goals (5)
  • Business requirements documented
  • Technical constraints identified
  • Research plan established
  • Scope boundaries defined
  • Stakeholder alignment confirmed
PM UX Research Engineering Stakeholders
Kickoff Meeting Phase 2

Cross-Functional Alignment

12
goals

All stakeholders align on scope, constraints, approach, and timeline. Open questions are documented, and everyone leaves with clarity on next steps.

► AI Augmentation
Human-Led
AI Handles
  • Pre-meeting context synthesis
  • Constraint mapping from technical docs
  • Draft agenda from discovery artifacts
Human Leads
  • Cross-functional alignment
  • Reading the room & relationship building
  • Real-time decision-making

"Alignment is a human act. AI can prepare the table — but can't read the room."

► Key Goals (5)
  • Requirements reviewed & confirmed
  • Design approach agreed upon
  • Timeline and goals set
  • Action items assigned
  • Next touchpoint scheduled
PM UX Design UX Research UX Engineer Engineering Marketing Merchandising
Design Phase Phase 3

Creation & Iteration

15
goals

UX Design creates solutions while UX Research validates assumptions. PM provides support and shields the team from scope creep.

► AI Augmentation
Human + AI
AI Handles
  • Generate 5-10 directions fast ("The Bad Build")
  • Code-based iteration via Claude Code
  • Volume at near-zero restart cost
  • Iterate against tokenized design system components
Human Leads
  • Creative direction — find sparks, cut noise
  • Figma craft in flow state
  • Novel solutions that can't be prompted

"Volume is AI. Taste is human. The craft moment is where novel solutions emerge."

► Key Goals (5)
  • User flows and UI designs
  • Research insights and validation
  • Promotional assets (if applicable)
  • Ad placements integrated
  • Design system components used
UX Design Lead UX Research PM Support UX Engineer Marketing Merchandising
Review & Handoff Phase 4

Validation & Preparation

19
goals

UX Engineer prepares dev-ready files and conducts handoff. Engineering reviews feasibility. All stakeholders approve before development begins.

► AI Augmentation
AI-Accelerated
AI Handles
  • Generate functional prototypes & dev-ready specs
  • Auto-document design decisions
  • Build handoff artifacts from Figma
Human Leads
  • Design QA & edge case review
  • Stakeholder approval
  • Catch what AI misses ("the seventh participant")

"Real users break things AI can't predict. QA is where judgment earns its keep."

► Key Goals (5)
  • Dev-ready specs and files
  • Design QA completed
  • Handoff meeting conducted
  • All approvals obtained
  • Open questions resolved
UX Engineer Lead Engineering UX Design PM Stakeholders
Post-Launch Phase 5

Measurement & Iteration

9
goals

UX Research measures success metrics and collects user feedback. The team captures lessons learned and uses insights to inform future iterations.

► AI Augmentation
Human + AI
AI Handles
  • Monitor success metrics & detect patterns
  • Synthesize user feedback at scale
  • Generate iteration candidates from data
Human Leads
  • Interpret what the numbers mean
  • Make strategic iteration calls
  • Document lessons for institutional memory

"AI shows you what happened. Humans decide what it means."

► Key Goals (5)
  • Success metrics monitored
  • User feedback collected
  • Post-launch research conducted
  • Lessons learned documented
  • Iteration plan created
